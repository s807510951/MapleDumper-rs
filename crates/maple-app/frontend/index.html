MapleDumper

                  
Cross-version signature and offset toolkit for MapleStory clients.

                  
Engine  · Ready

                

                

                  
MapleDumper finds, generates, and validates byte signatures and offsets that survive client patches. Feed it several client builds and it produces the highest-confidence pattern that resolves the same target in every version.

                  
    
                    
  • Cross-version Signature Maker with confidence grading.
    • 
                    
  • Assembly scan, pattern editor, and version history comparison.
    • 
                    
  • Privacy mask for safe screenshots and sharing.
